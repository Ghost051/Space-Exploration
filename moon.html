The Moon:

Facts/Data:

The Moon is Earth’s only natural satellite and the fifth largest moon in the solar system. The Moon’s presence helps stabilize our planet’s wobble and moderate our climate. The Moon has a very thin atmosphere called an exosphere. The Moon’s surface is cratered and pitted from comet and asteroid impacts.
